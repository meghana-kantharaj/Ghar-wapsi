What 'Ghar Wapsi' is all about

  A person's happiness depends upon how comfortable they are. Happiness comes from within and it comes only when someone is satisfied with their surroundings. But what when the life you had settled into is uprooted, when you have to move to a new place. Sure, adjusting takes time and you will get adjusted into your new life. It is hard, we know. So we are here to help!

Ghar Wapsi helps non-residents of India settle down in this beautiful place by making their life comfortable. We provide all the necessary amenities required for you. Plus, we organise weekend outings for like minded people based on their previous location. We suggest ideas that make it easy for you to live life the Indian way. Plus, we are here for you to help you out with any problem under the sun, so dont hesitate to reach out.

We do this for you, because life in India is way too beautiful to get lost among frivolity!
